Staff Timesheet — How to use it

For staff. Enter your hours each week — it takes a minute.

Open the app

Go to golden-lebkuchen-00bb4f.netlify.app

📱 On your phone: open the link, then tap Share → Add to Home Screen. It installs as the blue 🔵 app so you can open it like any other app.

1 · Sign in

Tap Sign in with Microsoft and use your normal work account. You only do this once on each device.

2 · Fill in your week & submit

Your site is set automatically. For each day you worked, add a work order and your hours, and tick any extras (e.g. Travel). Then tap Submit week.

Good to know

  • Start typing a work order and pick it from the list, or type your own.
  • You can re-open and change your week any time until your site's close day — just sign in and tap Submit.
  • If your supervisor sends a week back, you'll see a red note with the reason — fix it and Submit again.
  • Can't sign in, or wrong site? Use your work Microsoft account, or contact the office.
